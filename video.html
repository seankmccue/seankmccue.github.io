Broadcast Journalism

I created a series of monthly news packages for "Caver News Network" at San Diego High School. For each news package I reached out to interviewees, developed questions, shot interviews and B-Roll, wrote a script, recorded voice overs and used Adobe Premiere Pro to edit and create on screen graphics.
